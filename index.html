In 2015, 97% of cases filed against police officer(s) for suspected crimes did not result in the officer being charged with said crime.

Black people are more likely to be killed by the police. In fact, for every white person that is killed, 3 black people will be killed.

The statement "always following the rules is not compatible with getting the job done" was released to police to which 43% agreed.

61% of officers admitted that they do not always report the serious criminal violations of other fellow officers.
